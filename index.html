Hello/你好! I am an Assistant Professor in Statsitics at Georgia State University. I was a postdoctoral scholar at McGill University jointly with the University of Waterloo, kindly sponsored by the Canadian Statistical Sciences Institute (CANSSI). I am also affiliated with Mila and UW’s Health Data Science Lab. I was originally from Hsinchu (新竹) and Taipei (台北), Taiwan, and I lived in BC, Canada, for 12 years.
My office is: Room 1407, 25 Park Place.

Reserach Interests:

I possess a wide range of interests, but for now, my research is centred around:

  1. Functional data analysis.
  2. Statistical machine learning and high-dimensional statistical inference.
  3. Dependence modeling.
  4. Optimal design of experiment.
  5. Applications include drug development, toxicology, neuroscience, to only name a few.
