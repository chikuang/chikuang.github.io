About me

I am first year PhD student at the University of Waterloo under joint supervision of Prof. Greg Rice and Prof. Joel Dubin. I am also affiliated with Health Data Science Lab (HDSL). My other personal website hosted by UWaterloo server can be found here.

My current research focus is on Functional Data Analysis, particularly in Functional Time Series Analysis. I worked on optimal design of experiment problems before.

Before arriving to UWaterloo, I obtained MSc and BSc degrees at the University of Victoria in a beautiful city, Victoria, British Columbia, Canada.
