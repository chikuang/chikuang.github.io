Reserach Interests:

I possess a wide range of interests, but for now, my research is centred around:

  1. Functional data analysis, espeically with dependence
  2. Time series analysis
  3. Dependence modeling (both spatial and temporal)
  4. Optimal design of experiment
  5. Machine learning and high-dimensional data analysis
  6. Applications include biomedical, drug development and neuroscience
