About me: Hello. I am currently a CANSSI distinguish postdoctoral fellow at University of Waterloo & McGill University.

I completed Ph.D. at UWaterloo under the guidance of Professors Gregory Rice and Joel A. Dubin. During this time, I was affiliated with the Health Data Science Lab and also had privilege of working closely with Professor Peijun Sang. Prior to that, I obtained my BSc and MSc degrees from the University of Victoria where I was fortunate to work with Professor Julie Zhou.

I was originally from Hsinchu and Taipei, Taiwan, and I lived in BC, Canada, for 12 years.
